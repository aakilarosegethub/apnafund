Beloved musician and legendary saxophonist Ron Holloway has been a staple in the DC music scene for over 50 years. He has performed with Warren Haynes Band, Susan Tedeschi, Dizzy Gillespie, Gil Scott-Heron, Root Boy Slim, and many others. In 2014, he formed his own band, The Ron Holloway Band, which has been performing regularly throughout the region.

Ron's versatility spans across rock, jam, jazz, bluegrass, and funk genres. His image is immortalized in the D.C. Jazz Heroes Mural in D.C.'s Shaw Neighborhood, a testament to his lasting impact on the local music community.

This fundraiser aims to support Ron's medical expenses and ensure he can continue sharing his incredible talent with the world. Your donation will help cover treatment costs and allow him to focus on his recovery while maintaining his musical legacy.
